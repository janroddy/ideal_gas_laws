Ideal Gas Law

$PV=nRT$

Variables in the Ideal Gas Law equation

  • P - pressure - pascals (atm)
  • V - volume - liters (L)
  • P - pressure - pascals (atm)
  • P - pressure - pascals (atm)

Notes

  • only applies under ideal conditions
  • deviates from reality at low temperatures, high pressures, or high density
  • doesn't account for volume or attraction of particles
